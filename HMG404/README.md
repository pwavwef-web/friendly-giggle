# HMG404 — Professional and Leadership Development

> **University of Cape Coast | Level 400 | 2025/2026 Second Semester**
> Credit Hours: 3

---

## Course Description

This course focuses on the professional skills, ethical standards, and leadership competencies required for a successful career in hospitality and tourism management. It combines self-reflective practice with theoretical leadership frameworks to prepare students for industry roles.

## 📖 Key Topics

- Leadership styles and theories (transformational, servant, situational)
- Professional ethics and workplace conduct
- Career planning and personal branding
- Communication skills and presentation techniques
- Team dynamics and conflict resolution
- Reflective practice and continuous professional development (CPD)
- Mentoring and coaching in hospitality settings

---

## ✍️ Reflective Journal

The [`reflective-journal/`](./reflective-journal/) folder is used for weekly journal entries. Each entry should reflect on:
- What you learned this week (in class and independently)
- How it connects to your career goals
- What you will do differently or continue doing
