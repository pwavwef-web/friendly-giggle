# HMG402 — Strategic Management for Hospitality and Tourism

> **University of Cape Coast | Level 400 | 2025/2026 Second Semester**
> Credit Hours: 3

---

## Course Description

This course examines strategic management principles and their application in the hospitality and tourism industry. Topics include strategic analysis, competitive positioning, strategic formulation and implementation, and performance evaluation in hospitality and tourism organisations.

## 📖 Key Topics

- Strategic analysis tools (SWOT, PESTLE, Porter's Five Forces)
- Business-level and corporate-level strategies
- Strategic alliances and mergers in hospitality
- Strategy implementation and change management
- Performance measurement frameworks (Balanced Scorecard)
- Case studies from international and Ghanaian hospitality firms

- Olsen, M. D., West, J., & Tse, E. C. Y. (2008). *Strategic Management in the Hospitality Industry*. Pearson.
