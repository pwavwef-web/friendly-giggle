# TMG499 — Project Work (Dissertation)

> **University of Cape Coast | Level 400 | 2025/2026 Second Semester**
> Credit Hours: 6

---

## Course Description

TMG499 is the capstone research project for the BSc Tourism Management programme. Students independently conduct original research under faculty supervision, applying knowledge and skills developed throughout the programme to produce a full academic dissertation on a topic relevant to tourism management.
