## 🗓️ 16-Week Milestone Checklist

### Phase 1 — Preparation (Weeks 1–3)

- [ ] **Week 1:** Confirm research topic area and meet supervisor for the first time
- [ ] **Week 1:** Review UCC dissertation guidelines and formatting requirements
- [ ] **Week 2:** Conduct initial literature scan (Google Scholar, UCC Library, AJOL)
- [ ] **Week 2:** Narrow down research topic and formulate a working title
- [ ] **Week 3:** Draft research problem statement, objectives, and research questions
- [ ] **Week 3:** Submit initial topic/title to supervisor for approval

### Phase 2 — Proposal (Weeks 4–5)

- [ ] **Week 4:** Write Chapter 1 draft — Introduction, Background, Problem Statement
- [ ] **Week 4:** Outline Chapter 2 — Literature Review structure
- [ ] **Week 5:** Write and submit full research proposal
- [ ] **Week 5:** Revise proposal based on supervisor feedback and resubmit if required

### Phase 3 — Literature Review (Weeks 6–7)

- [ ] **Week 6:** Conduct deep literature review — collect and read at least 30 sources
- [ ] **Week 6:** Set up Zotero library and organise references by theme
- [ ] **Week 7:** Write Chapter 2 — Literature Review (first draft)
- [ ] **Week 7:** Submit Chapter 2 draft to supervisor for feedback

### Phase 4 — Methodology (Week 8)

- [ ] **Week 8:** Design research methodology (qualitative / quantitative / mixed)
- [ ] **Week 8:** Develop data collection instruments (questionnaire / interview guide)
- [ ] **Week 8:** Write Chapter 3 — Methodology (first draft)
- [ ] **Week 8:** Seek ethical approval if required by the department

### Phase 5 — Data Collection (Weeks 9–10)

- [ ] **Week 9:** Pilot test data collection instruments and refine
- [ ] **Week 9:** Begin primary data collection
- [ ] **Week 10:** Complete data collection and organise raw data files
- [ ] **Week 10:** Submit Chapter 3 — Methodology (revised) to supervisor

### Phase 6 — Data Analysis (Weeks 11–12)

- [ ] **Week 11:** Analyse collected data (using SPSS / NVivo / manual coding as appropriate)
- [ ] **Week 11:** Generate charts, tables, and statistical outputs
- [ ] **Week 12:** Write Chapter 4 — Results / Findings (first draft)
- [ ] **Week 12:** Review and discuss findings with supervisor

### Phase 7 — Discussion & Conclusion (Weeks 13–14)

- [ ] **Week 13:** Write Chapter 5 — Discussion of Findings
- [ ] **Week 13:** Link findings back to the literature review and theoretical framework
- [ ] **Week 14:** Write Chapter 6 — Conclusion, Recommendations, and Limitations
- [ ] **Week 14:** Complete References section using Zotero / APA 7th Edition

### Phase 8 — Revision & Submission (Weeks 15–16)

- [ ] **Week 15:** Compile all chapters into a single document
- [ ] **Week 15:** Proofread entire dissertation for grammar, spelling, and consistency
- [ ] **Week 15:** Run plagiarism check via Turnitin (access through UCC Library)
- [ ] **Week 15:** Submit complete draft to supervisor for final review
- [ ] **Week 16:** Implement all final supervisor corrections
- [ ] **Week 16:** Format dissertation according to UCC guidelines (title page, TOC, pagination)
- [ ] **Week 16:** Submit final dissertation (hard copy and/or digital) by deadline
- [ ] **Week 16:** Archive all research materials in this repository

## 💡 Research Topic Ideas (Ghana's Tourism Industry)

The following are potential research areas aligned with Ghana's tourism context and current academic discourse:

### Sustainable & Ecotourism
- The role of community-based ecotourism in poverty reduction in Ghana's forest zones
- Visitor perceptions of sustainability practices at Kakum National Park
- Challenges and prospects of ecotourism development in the Volta Region

### Cultural & Heritage Tourism
- The impact of diaspora tourism (Year of Return / Beyond the Return) on Ghana's economy
- Community engagement in heritage site management: A study of Cape Coast and Elmina Castles
- Dark tourism and its role in preserving Ghana's slave trade history

### Transport & Accessibility
- The effect of domestic air transport on regional tourism development in Ghana
- Road infrastructure challenges as a barrier to tourism growth in northern Ghana

### Digital & Contemporary Tourism
- Social media influence on tourism destination choice among Ghanaian millennials
- The impact of COVID-19 on Ghana's tourism sector and strategies for recovery
- Overtourism and carrying capacity management at popular Ghanaian destinations

### Hospitality & Management
- Customer satisfaction and service quality in Accra's hotel sector
- Human resource development challenges in Ghana's hospitality industry
- The role of strategic management in the survival of small tourism enterprises in Ghana
