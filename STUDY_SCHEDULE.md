## 📝 Daily Study Routine Breakdown

### Morning Review (06:00 – 07:00)
- Review previous day's lecture notes (15 min)
- Read ahead for today's class (30 min)
- Check emails and academic notices (15 min)

### Class Time (08:00 – 12:00)
- Attend all scheduled lectures and tutorials
- Take structured notes using Cornell or mind-map method
- Note down key questions for follow-up

### Afternoon Study (13:00 – 17:00)
- Complete assigned readings and exercises
- Work on assignments or coursework
- Dedicate at least **2 hours to dissertation work** on Wednesdays and Saturdays

### Evening Review (18:00 – 21:00)
- Consolidate and review notes taken during the day
- Read relevant journal articles or textbook chapters
- Update reflective journal (HMG404) and dissertation log
