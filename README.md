## 🎯 Study Goals

- [ ] Achieve a minimum of B+ in all taught courses
- [ ] Submit a well-researched and well-written dissertation (TMG499) by the deadline
- [ ] Develop professional and leadership skills applicable to the tourism industry (HMG404)
- [ ] Analyse contemporary tourism issues relevant to Ghana and sub-Saharan Africa (TMG406)
- [ ] Build a strong understanding of sustainable ecotourism principles (TMG412)
- [ ] Apply strategic management frameworks to real-world hospitality scenarios (HMG402)
- [ ] Complete all assignments and submit before deadlines
- [ ] Attend all scheduled supervisor meetings for the dissertation
- [ ] Maintain an up-to-date reflective journal throughout the semester
