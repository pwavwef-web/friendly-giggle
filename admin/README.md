# 📁 Admin — Administrative Documents

> University of Cape Coast | Level 400 | 2025/2026 Second Semester

This folder contains administrative documents for the semester, including the academic timetable, course registration records, and past examination questions used for revision.

---

## Subfolder Guide

| Subfolder | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| [`timetables/`](./timetables/) | Official UCC lecture and exam timetables |
| [`registration-docs/`](./registration-docs/) | Course registration confirmation, fee receipts |
| [`past-exam-questions/`](./past-exam-questions/) | Past exam questions for all Level 400 courses |

---

## 💡 Tips

- Download your official timetable from the [UCC Student Portal](https://sts.ucc.edu.gh) and save it here.
- Keep a scanned copy of your registration slip for reference.
- Organise past exam questions by course code (e.g. `HMG402_2023_exam.pdf`).
