# TMG410 — Transport Management

> **University of Cape Coast | Level 400 | 2025/2026 Second Semester**
> Credit Hours: 3

---

## Course Description

This course explores the role of transport systems in the development and sustainability of tourism. It covers air, road, rail, and sea transport, along with transport policy, infrastructure challenges, and the relationship between mobility and tourist destination development, with a focus on Ghana and West Africa.

## 📖 Key Topics

- The role of transport in tourism development
- Air transport: airlines, airports, and aviation policy
- Road transport and its impact on destination accessibility
- Rail and water transport in tourism contexts
- Transport planning and infrastructure development
- Low-cost carriers and tourism democratisation
- Ghana's transport infrastructure and tourism implications
- Sustainable transport and carbon footprint reduction
- IATA standards and international aviation regulations
