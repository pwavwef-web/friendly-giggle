# HMG406 — Facilities Management

> **University of Cape Coast | Level 400 | 2025/2026 Second Semester**
> Credit Hours: 3

---

## Course Description

This course covers the principles and practices of managing physical facilities in the hospitality and tourism industry. It addresses the planning, operation, maintenance, and sustainability of hospitality facilities, including hotels, resorts, and event venues.

## 📖 Key Topics

- Overview of facilities management in hospitality
- Building systems: HVAC, electrical, plumbing, and fire safety
- Maintenance planning (preventive, corrective, and predictive)
- Energy management and environmental sustainability
- Space planning and interior design considerations
- Health, safety, and legal compliance
- Technology in modern facilities management (BMS, CAFM systems)
