# TMG412 — Ecotourism

> **University of Cape Coast | Level 400 | 2025/2026 Second Semester**
> Credit Hours: 3

---

## Course Description

This course examines ecotourism as a form of sustainable tourism that focuses on natural environments, biodiversity conservation, and community benefit. Students explore ecotourism theory, planning, management, and best practices, with particular attention to Ghana's national parks, forest reserves, and coastal ecosystems.

## 📖 Key Topics

- Definition, principles, and evolution of ecotourism
- Theoretical frameworks: sustainable development, carrying capacity
- Ecotourism planning and management
- Community-based ecotourism (CBET)
- Wildlife tourism and conservation financing
- Certification and standards (e.g. TIES, Green Globe)
- Ecotourism in Ghana: Kakum National Park, Mole National Park, Volta Region
- Impacts of ecotourism: economic, social, cultural, and environmental
- Challenges and critiques of ecotourism
