# TMG406 — Contemporary Issues in Tourism

> **University of Cape Coast | Level 400 | 2025/2026 Second Semester**
> Credit Hours: 3

---

## Course Description

This course examines emerging and evolving issues shaping the global tourism industry. Students critically analyse contemporary challenges and opportunities including technological disruption, overtourism, post-pandemic recovery, climate change, and shifting tourist behaviour, with a focus on implications for Africa and Ghana.

## 📖 Key Topics

- Post-COVID-19 tourism recovery strategies
- Overtourism and destination management
- Digital transformation: AI, big data, and smart tourism
- Climate change and sustainable tourism
- Diaspora and cultural tourism (e.g. Ghana's Year of Return)
- Dark tourism and thanatourism
- Voluntourism and ethical tourism practices
- Inclusive and accessible tourism
- LGBTQ+ tourism and social dimensions
